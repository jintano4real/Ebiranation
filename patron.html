Pharmacist Abdulkareem Moh'd Jamiu Asuku

Grand Patron, Ebira Nation

  • Pharm. Abdulkareem Asuku Youth Empowerment Foundation (PAAYEF).
  • Pharmacist Abdulkareem Moh'd Jamiu Asuku is an ardent lover of progressive minds and operate an open-door policy for easy assessment to citizens of Kogi state regardless of their ethnic group.
  • His superb shows of leadership strength transcended into humanitarian acts and thereby making him become one of the most celebrated appointees of the new direction of government and a philanthropist in the state.
  • Pharm. Asuka is known for his impactful humanitarian acts, from Youth Empowerments, Skill Acquisitions, Educational Outreaches, Healthcare Interventions and Human Capital Development to mention a few.
  • His disposition to humanity and numerous acts of kindness is widely acknowledged, as he has through his foundation, Pharm. Abdulkareem Asuku Youth Empowerment Foundation (PAAYEF) catered for the health and well-being of many.
  • The foundation has also, over the past few years granted hundreds of scholarships to students to study till the conclusion of their tertiary education. He has taken responsibility for hundreds of students across the state with payment of school fees, as well as WAEC, NECO, and JAMB fees. Many students in Kogi State are currently in school today, all thanks to PAAYEF.
  • Pharm. Asuka has empowered young Men and Women with the required skills and start-up funds; in fashion design, and the acquisition of digital and technical skills to name but a few. Indeed he is a leader with voluminous track record in the field of humanity.
  • He became the Grand Patron of Ebira Nations on December 16, 2022.
